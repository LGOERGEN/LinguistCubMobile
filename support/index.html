Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new child profile?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new child profile. You can customize their name and select which languages they're learning.
Can I track multiple languages for one child?
Yes! Linguist Cub supports multilingual tracking. Select multiple languages in your child's profile to track their vocabulary development across all languages.
How do I mark words as 'Understanding' vs 'Speaking'?
When adding or editing a word, you'll see two buttons. Tap "Understands" if your child comprehends the word, and "Speaks" if they can say it aloud.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. All your child's data is stored securely and privately. We never share personal information with third parties.
Can I export my child's language data?
Yes, you can export your child's vocabulary progress and statistics from the app settings menu.
The app won't sync between devices. What should I do?
Make sure you're logged into the same account on both devices and have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact support.
